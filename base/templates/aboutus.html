Our Company

At Kaffa, we’re more than just a coffee shop — we’re a celebration of origin, culture, and community. Inspired by the Ethiopian region of Kaffa, the legendary birthplace of coffee, our mission is to honor the roots of this timeless drink while creating a space where people can slow down, connect, and savor the moment.


Our journey began with a passion for rich flavors and authentic storytelling. Every bean we brew is carefully selected to reflect the bold spirit and deep heritage of Ethiopian coffee. From single-origin classics to modern blends, our menu is curated to take you on a sensory journey — one cup at a time.


But Kaffa isn’t just about what’s in your mug. It’s about the experience that surrounds it — the welcoming aroma when you walk in, the thoughtful conversations over cappuccino, and the care in every pour. We believe in crafting not only exceptional coffee but also meaningful moments.


Whether you’re a curious explorer, a coffee aficionado, or simply in search of your daily ritual, Kaffa invites you to discover the origins, taste the tradition, and feel right at home.
